# TransAlert

I made this because the buses in my city didn't have (and still don't have) stop announcements, and when starting highschool I also had to start using them daily if I wanted to be able to get to school by myself.

TransAlert is an app that allows blind people to use public transport independently. Using your location, it can help you navigate by telling you what stop are you currently on, how much meters you have left until arrival and notifying you when you get to your destination.
